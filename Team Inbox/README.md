# Team Inbox

This is where the owner places tasks, files, and resources for the team to access.

## Purpose
The owner drops tasks, instructions, files, and images here for the team to pick up and act on.

## How It Works
1. Owner places a task, file, or image in this folder
2. The orchestrator picks it up and routes it to the appropriate team member
3. The team member works on it and delivers results to the Owner Inbox

## What Goes Here
- Task briefs and instructions
- Files and images for the team to work with
- Research requests
- New hire requests
